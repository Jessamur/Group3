KYFP Spring 2022 Youth Crew - BARK

This Spring, B.A.R.K. (Building a Resilient Kingston), a project of the Kingston YMCA Farm Project, has employed 10 high-school aged teenagers to enage in local community science and other forms of community engagment throughout Kingston. BARK supervisors will be three Empowerment Coordinators who will take on the leadership role for facilitating engagment and organizing programming for this session.
